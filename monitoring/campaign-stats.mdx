title: Campaign stats
description: Track a campaign's progress, outcomes, and calls.
---

Each campaign's detail page shows how it's progressing and how its calls have been resolving.

## Campaign summary

At a glance, see the total number of contacts, how many have been processed, total call minutes, and overall progress.

## Outcome breakdown

See how the campaign's calls have broken down by [call outcome](/build-voice-agents/call-outcomes) — for example, how many reached voicemail versus how many connected.

## Call log panel

A call log scoped to the campaign shows the individual calls behind those numbers. See [Call logs](/monitoring/call-logs) for the full call log view, including detail and export.
