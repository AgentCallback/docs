Create an AgentCallback account to set up a new organization and begin building voice agents.

## Before you begin

Have an organization name, an email address, and a password ready. If you are joining an existing organization, ask an owner to invite you instead of creating a second organization.

## Create your account

1. Open the [AgentCallback sign-up page](https://agentcallback.ai/sign-up).
2. Enter your **Organization Name**. This name identifies your team's AgentCallback account.
3. Enter your **Email** and choose a **Password**.
4. Read and accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
5. Select **Sign Up**.
6. Open the verification email from AgentCallback and select its verification link.
7. Return to AgentCallback and sign in.

<Note>
If you do not receive the verification email, use **Resend verification email** on the confirmation screen. Check your spam folder before requesting another email.
</Note>

## Sign up with Google

You can also select **Sign in with Google** on the sign-in page. Complete the Google authorization flow, then return to AgentCallback to finish entering your organization details if prompted.

## Join an existing organization

Do not create a new organization when a teammate has already set one up. Ask an owner to invite the email address you use for AgentCallback. Open the invitation link, create or sign in to your account, and accept the invitation.

<Warning>
Organization names must be unique. If the name you enter already exists, use a different name or ask that organization's owner for an invitation.
</Warning>
