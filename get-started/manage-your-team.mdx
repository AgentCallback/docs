---
title: Manage your team
description: Invite teammates, assign roles, and manage pending invitations.
---

Owners can invite teammates and manage member access from the **Teams** page.

## Invite a teammate

1. In the dashboard sidebar, select **Teams**.
2. Select **Invite Teammate**.
3. Enter the teammate's email address.
4. Choose a role:
   - **Team Member** can manage their own campaigns and view shared agents, phone numbers, and voices.
   - **Owner** has full access, including billing, API keys, and team management.
5. Select **Send Invite**.
6. Ask your teammate to open the invitation email and accept the invitation.

<Note>
Invite a person using the same email address they will use to sign in. An email address that is already a member or has a pending invitation cannot receive another new invitation.
</Note>

## Resend a pending invitation

1. Open **Teams**.
2. Find the person with a pending invitation.
3. Select **Resend**.
4. Confirm that you want to send a new invitation.

The previous invitation link stops working when you resend it. The page may temporarily show a resend countdown before another resend is available.

## Change a member's role

1. Open **Teams**.
2. Find the active team member.
3. Select the role dropdown in their row.
4. Choose **Owner** or **Team Member**.

Role changes apply immediately. Keep at least one owner in the organization so someone can manage access and billing.
