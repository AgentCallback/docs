# AgentCallback documentation

AgentCallback helps you build AI voice agents for inbound and outbound calls. Start by creating your organization, inviting the people who will operate it, and building your first agent.

## Your first workflow

1. [Sign up for AgentCallback](/get-started/sign-up) and verify your email.
2. [Invite your team](/get-started/manage-your-team) when you are ready to collaborate.
3. [Create a voice agent](/get-started/create-a-voice-agent) and review its first test.
