Browse AgentCallback's voice catalog and your organization's own cloned voices, and preview each one before assigning it to an agent.

## Browse voices

Open the voice library to see the public voice catalog alongside any private or cloned voices your organization has created. Filter by attributes like gender and language to narrow down the list.

## Preview a voice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a voice">
    Find a voice in the library.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play a sample">
    Select the play button to hear a sample of the voice before choosing it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assign a voice to an agent

Once you've found a voice you like, select it from your agent's configuration so calls from that agent use it.
